Features

Vehicle Control

  • Remote start and climate pre-conditioning (vehicles before 2019)
  • Battery heater for cold-weather range
  • Charge management and scheduling
  • Remote door and window operation
  • Engine control (ON / OFF / Release)
  • Module reset and DTC clearing

Professional Diagnostics

  • Deep DTC scan across all modules
  • 100+ live parameters with dual-column mode
  • Real-time battery pack analysis: cell voltages, temps
  • Weak Cell active test with guided verdict
  • Battery coolant pump and heater control
  • HW Info with module programming dates

Live Monitoring

Nine dedicated screens, every value charted in real time:

  • Efficiency — SoC, EV range, kWh, fuel efficiency
  • Powertrain — HV power, motor temps, transmission
  • HV Battery — pack voltage, cell delta, temps, coolant
  • Charger — AC/DC voltage, current, power, duty cycles
  • Climate — HVAC, cabin heat, AC, blower, remote start
  • 12V — voltage, current, power, per-module supply
  • TPMS — live tire pressure for all four wheels

HV Unlock

"Unbrick" your car and stay on the road — avoid a tow truck by resetting the high voltage system right from your phone. Clears Service High Voltage Charging System faults and re-enables driving and charging.

Health Insights

Critical vehicle health analysis and maintenance recommendations — ideal for DIY enthusiasts, used car buyers checking battery health, and owners tracking long-term performance.

History & Export

  • Full history view with vehicle selection
  • Export, import, and edit vehicle history

Polished Experience

  • Dark / Light / System theme
  • Multi-language support
  • Bottom status bar for at-a-glance info
